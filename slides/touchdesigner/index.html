TouchDesigner

TouchDesigner is a node based visual programming language for real time interactive multimedia content, developed by the Toronto-based company Derivative.

Nodes

a data point or cluster within a tree or other information structure, as defined by its relationship to another data point or cluster.

Nodes

Nodes are the building blocks of TouchDesigner. They are the fundamental unit of computation and data flow.
